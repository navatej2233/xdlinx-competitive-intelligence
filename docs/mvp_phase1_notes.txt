MVP Phase 1 Overview

Scope:
- Region: United States
- Competitors: Planet Labs, Spire Global, BlackSky
- Features:
  • Competitor news via Google News RSS
  • Stock price trend via Yahoo Finance
  • Buy / Hold / Sell indicator based on analyst recommendations

Data Refresh:
- News and stock data are refreshed on app load
- Free-tier public data sources used
- No real-time guarantees

Limitations:
- Limited historical depth
- Analyst recommendations may not be available for all competitors
- Single-competitor stock view (by design for MVP)

Next Steps:
- Expand competitor coverage
- Improve insight summaries
- Add region-based comparisons

Competitor Handling:
- Public companies include news, stock trends, and analyst sentiment
- Private companies are limited to news coverage due to data availability
